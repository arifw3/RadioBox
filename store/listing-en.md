# Radio Box — Store Listing (English)

## App Name (30 char limit)
Radio Box - Live Radio Streaming

## Short Description (80 char limit)
Thousands of live radio stations, drive mode, sleep timer — 100% free.

## Full Description (4000 char limit)

**Listen to live radio from around the world with Radio Box — no account, no fees, just radio.**

From Turkey's most-loved stations to thousands of live broadcasts worldwide, everything is one tap away. Radio Box was built from scratch to fix the classic problems of radio apps: playback stopping in the background, dead links, and clunky interfaces.

📻 **Thousands of Stations, Always Fresh**
A large station library that's automatically scanned and health-checked every night. Broken links get cleaned up automatically, so you always see stations that actually work.

🎵 **What's Playing Right Now?**
Automatically finds the artist name, cover art, and song title for whatever's on air and shows it on screen — even when the station itself doesn't broadcast that info.

🚗 **Drive Mode**
Detects when you've gotten in a car (or switch it on manually) and turns the whole screen into one giant, single-touch control panel. Swipe left/right to change stations, swipe up/down for volume — without ever taking your eyes off the road.

😴 **Sleep Timer**
Fall asleep to the radio — the app fades the volume down and stops playback for you automatically.

⏪ **Time Shift**
Rewind live playback up to 10–15 minutes — catch whatever you just missed.

📴 **When the Connection Drops**
Automatically reconnects on a weak signal; if it drops completely, a few built-in royalty-free ambient tracks kick in until the radio comes back.

🔍 **Voice or Text Search**
Find the exact station you want among thousands, in seconds — by speaking or typing.

⭐ **Favorites & Song History**
Save the stations you love, and see a history of every song you've listened to.

🎨 **Dynamic Design**
The app's colors shift live to match whatever station's logo is playing. A Winamp-style circular visualizer turns listening into a real show.

📤 **Share**
Share the song you're listening to as a stylish story card on Instagram and WhatsApp.

💯 **Completely Free, Forever**
No subscriptions, no in-app purchases, no intrusive full-screen or audio ads. Just one small, unobtrusive banner at the bottom of the screen — that's it.

Download Radio Box and turn your phone into a world radio.
